The Bajio

Fishing Performance Cloaked In Style

A pant built for the dock but tailored for downtown. Cut in a modern five-pocket/chino silhouette, it wears like your favorite dress casual yet works like your go-to fishing gear. The lightweight stretch fabric dries fast, breathes in the Southern heat, and shrugs off salt, sun, and stains with a PFC-free finish. Hidden gussets and articulated knees move with you, while discreet details—like a microfiber-lined pocket for glasses or phone, a tool sleeve tucked in the seam, and glare-free matte buttons that won’t flash in the sun—keep performance at hand without sacrificing style. From boat to boardroom to backyard, this is fishing performance cloaked in timeless design.

The Eastern Luxe Cashmere Five-Pocket

Extreme luxury redefined

This versatile five-pocket pant (like all our bottoms) is crafted from a cashmere-rich blend that’s soft, breathable, and seasonless—perfect from New England winters to Carolina summers. Tailored with a modern, versatile fit, it features discreet performance touches like a microfiber-lined pocket for eyewear or phone. A highly refined staple built to move seamlessly along the eastern latitudes.
